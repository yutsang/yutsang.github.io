About關於

My strengths sit at the intersection of finance and data science: statistical modeling, machine learning where it fits, and analytics pipelines that support investment, risk, and operational decisions. I care about defensible methods, reproducible analysis, and communication that non-specialists can act on. 我的專長在金融與資料科學的交集：統計建模、在合適情境使用機器學習，以及支援投資、風險與營運決策的分析流程。我重視可被檢視的方法、可重現的分析，以及非專業人士也能據以行動的表達方式。

I'm particularly enthusiastic about operations research—linear and discrete optimization, scheduling and routing, simulation, and using quantitative models to improve how organizations allocate resources. I also build interactive dashboards when they help stakeholders explore data and align on trade-offs. 我特別熱愛作業研究——線性與離散最佳化、排程與路徑規劃、模擬，以及運用數量模型改善組織的資源配置。當互動式儀表板能幫助各方探索資料並對取捨達成共識時，我也會主動建置。

Outside of day-to-day work I read broadly across quantitative finance, ML research, and systems design, and I enjoy writing up what I learn in the insights section of this site. 工作以外，我會廣泛閱讀量化金融、機器學習研究與系統設計相關內容，並把學到的東西寫進本站的「觀察」。

Get in touch聯絡我

I'm always interested in new opportunities and exciting projects. Whether you have a question, a collaboration idea, or just want to say hi, feel free to reach out. 我一直樂於接觸新的機會與有趣的專案。無論是問題、合作想法，或只是想打個招呼，都歡迎隨時聯絡。

Email is the fastest way to reach me for anything that needs a written response. For quick introductions or networking, LinkedIn is fine too. 若需要文字回覆，電郵是最快的聯繫方式；若只是簡短自我介紹或人脈交流，LinkedIn 也很合適。
