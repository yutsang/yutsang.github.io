§ 01 / About · Bio關於 · 簡介

About.關於。

I'm Yu, a data scientist in Hong Kong working in professional services. My work sits at the intersection of finance and data science: statistical modeling, machine learning where it genuinely fits, and analytics pipelines that support investment, risk, and operational decisions. I care about defensible methods, reproducible analysis, and communication that non-specialists can act on. 我是 Yu，在香港專業服務業擔任數據科學家。我的工作在金融與數據科學的交集：統計建模、在真正合適的情境使用機器學習，以及支援投資、風險與營運決策的分析流程。我重視可被檢視的方法、可重現的分析，以及非專業人士也能據以行動的表達方式。

I trained as an industrial engineer at HKUST — a BEng in Industrial Engineering & Engineering Management followed by an MSc in Enterprise Engineering Management — and started out in banking with DBS's Treasury & Markets team. That background is why I'm particularly enthusiastic about operations research: linear and discrete optimization, scheduling and routing, simulation, and using quantitative models to improve how organizations allocate resources. 我在香港科技大學受工業工程訓練——先完成工業工程及工程管理學工學士，再修讀企業工程管理理學碩士——並在 DBS 銀行 Treasury & Markets 團隊展開職涯。這樣的背景使我特別熱愛運籌學：線性與離散優化、排程與路徑規劃、模擬，以及運用數量模型改善組織的資源配置。

Outside of day-to-day work I read broadly across quantitative finance, ML research, and systems design, build open tools on Hong Kong open data, and write up what I learn in the insights section of this site. 工作以外，我廣泛閱讀量化金融、機器學習研究與系統設計相關內容，也用香港開放數據打造開放工具，並把學到的東西寫進本站的「觀察」。

Everything on this site is personal and independent — views, projects, and writing are my own, built on public data, and don't represent any employer or client. 本站所有內容純屬個人、獨立製作——觀點、專案與文章皆為個人所有，僅使用公開數據，不代表任何僱主或客戶立場。
