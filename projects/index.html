HK Last-Mile ETA Prediction香港最後一里 ETA 預測

Final-year project with LF Logistics: an XGBoost model that predicts delivery delay ratio (actual vs planned time) instead of raw duration, trained on 65,330 real HK delivery segments. Key finding: vehicle type and origin depot beat distance as predictors. Ships with a Streamlit analytics dashboard. 與 LF Logistics 合作的畢業專題：以 XGBoost 預測配送的 delay ratio（實際 vs 計劃時間）而非原始時長，用 65,330 條真實香港配送紀錄訓練。核心發現：車型與出發倉庫比距離更有預測力。附 Streamlit 分析 dashboard。
