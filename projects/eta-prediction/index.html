HK Last-Mile ETA Prediction香港最後一里 ETA 預測

Predicting delivery delay, not delivery duration — final-year project with LF Logistics 預測配送延誤，而非配送時長本身——與 LF Logistics 合作的畢業專題

§ 01 / The problem問題

Traffic data you already have你早已擁有的交通數據

Repo on GitHub →GitHub 源碼 →

Hong Kong doesn't have a clean, public, city-wide traffic-condition dataset. What it does have — in abundance, inside every logistics operator's systems — is last-mile delivery records: timestamps, routes, vehicle types, planned versus actual arrival. This project treats those commercial records as a proxy for urban traffic: if congestion is real, it should show up as systematic delay in deliveries that have nothing to do with traffic on paper. 香港沒有一個乾淨、公開、覆蓋全城的交通狀況數據集。但每間物流公司的系統內，都大量存在最後一里配送紀錄：時間戳記、路線、車種、計劃與實際到達時間。這個專題把這些商業紀錄當成城市交通的代理指標——如果塞車是真實存在的，它應該會系統性地反映在配送延誤上，即使紀錄本身從未提及「交通」二字。

The key design choice: predict the delay ratio — actual duration divided by planned duration — instead of raw delivery time. Raw duration is dominated by route length, which has nothing to do with congestion. The ratio removes that trivial effect and isolates the part of the signal that's actually about operational and traffic-driven variance. 關鍵的設計選擇：預測延誤比率（實際時長除以計劃時長），而非原始配送時間。原始時長主要由路線長度決定，與塞車與否無關；延誤比率剔除了這個顯而易見的因素，留下真正與營運及交通相關的變異。

§ 02 / The data數據

Anonymised, multi-city, mostly Hong Kong匿名化、跨城市、以香港為主

The source is anonymised last-mile logistics records from a regional Southeast Asian operator (2019–2020), covering Hong Kong as the primary market alongside China, Singapore, Thailand, Indonesia, Malaysia, the Philippines, and Taiwan. Deliveries ran out of four Hong Kong warehouses (OBW0, TSW0, TSX0, OBWL) using a mix of 9-tonne trucks, vans, and 16-tonne trucks — 9-tonne trucks dominate the fleet. 數據來源是東南亞區域物流商的匿名化最後一里紀錄（2019–2020），以香港為主要市場，並涵蓋中國、新加坡、泰國、印尼、馬來西亞、菲律賓與台灣。香港的配送由四個倉庫（OBW0、TSW0、TSX0、OBWL）發出，車型包括 9 噸貨車、客貨車與 16 噸貨車——9 噸貨車佔車隊主力。

Because the data was never collected with traffic analysis in mind, cleaning was most of the work — 85% of raw Hong Kong segments were missing an actual completion time, which is itself a finding about how logistics data quality degrades at the edges of a system not designed for downstream analytics. The 9,950 segments that survived cleaning are what the model was trained and evaluated on. 由於數據原本從未為交通分析而設計，清理本身就是大部分工作量——85% 的香港原始配送段缺失實際完成時間，這本身就是一個發現：說明物流數據品質在一個並非為下游分析而設計的系統邊緣會如何劣化。清理後留下的 9,950 段配送紀錄，就是模型訓練與評估所用的數據。

§ 03 / What the model found模型的發現

Operations beat geography營運因素勝過地理因素

An XGBoost model trained on the delay ratio target found that vehicle category and origin depot are stronger predictors of delay than distance or time-of-day — the two variables most people would guess first. That's a genuinely useful, slightly counterintuitive result: it suggests that operational factors (which depot a delivery starts from, what kind of vehicle carries it) dominate over pure road-network traffic effects in this dataset, at least at the level this data can resolve. 以延誤比率為目標訓練的 XGBoost 模型發現，車型與出發倉庫比距離或時段更能預測延誤——後兩者通常是大多數人第一直覺會猜的變數。這是一個真正有用、略為反直覺的結果：它顯示在這個數據集所能解析的層面上，營運因素（配送由哪個倉庫出發、由哪種車輛承運）比純粹的路網交通效應更具主導性。

Reading this finding correctly: delivery delay ratios encode time-of-day congestion signals even though the data was never collected as traffic data — but the strongest predictors turned out to be operational, not geographic. That's a claim about this specific fleet and period, not a general statement about Hong Kong traffic; it's exactly the kind of result that's only visible once you stop assuming raw duration is the right target. 正確理解這個發現：配送延誤比率確實編碼了時段性擁堵訊號，即使數據從未以交通數據的身分被收集——但最強的預測因子最終是營運性的，而非地理性的。這是針對這支特定車隊與這段時期的觀察，不是對香港交通的普遍論斷；這正是一種只有在放棄「原始時長就是正確目標」這個假設之後，才會浮現的發現。
§ 04 / Stack & status技術棧與現況

Try it yourself親自試試

Built in Python with XGBoost for modelling and a Streamlit app for exploring predictions interactively. The underlying delivery records are commercial and anonymised, so they aren't published — the repo ships the full pipeline (cleaning, feature engineering, training, evaluation) and the Streamlit dashboard code, so the modelling approach is fully reproducible even without the original data. 以 Python 搭配 XGBoost 建模，並以 Streamlit 應用互動式探索預測結果。原始配送紀錄屬商業數據且已匿名化，因此不會公開發布——repo 內公開了完整流程（清理、特徵工程、訓練、評估）與 Streamlit dashboard 程式碼，即使沒有原始數據，建模方法本身仍完全可重現。
